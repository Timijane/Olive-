Dining & Restaurants

Olive Hotel & Suites celebrates culinary variety — from fine dining to casual bites. Our outlets include:

  • Signature Dining: Gourmet international menu with seasonal tasting menus.
  • The Olive Terrace: All-day dining featuring local and continental favorites.
  • Rooftop Lounge: Cocktails, lounge plates and skyline views.
  • In-room Dining: 24/7 full-service dining delivered to your suite.
  • Private chef experiences: Book an intimate private dining event.

We accommodate dietary needs — vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free options available.
